Lab 3: Text Classification

For this lab excercise, you will work with the IMDb Movie Reviews dataset.This is a 
binary sentiment analysis dataset consisting of 50,000 reviews from the Internet Movie Database (IMDb) labeled as positive or negative. The dataset contains an even number of positive and negative reviews.

You can download the IMDb dataset in csv format from the link below:

URL: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/lakshmi25npathi/imdb-dataset-of-50k-movie-reviews/data
